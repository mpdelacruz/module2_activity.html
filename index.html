About FEU Institute of Technology

FEU Institute of Technology (FEU Tech) is one of the leading technology schools in the Philippines. It offers innovative programs in engineering, computer studies, and multimedia arts.

The school provides modern facilities, strong academic foundations, and an industry-driven curriculum that prepares graduates for global opportunities.

Academic Programs

  • Engineering (Civil, Computer, Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical)
  • Computer Studies (Computer Science, Information Technology)
  • Multimedia Arts and Animation
  • Business Administration

Student Organizations

FEU Tech offers a wide range of student organizations that allow students to grow academically, socially, and creatively:

  • Association of Civil Engineering Students
  • Alliance of Information Technology Students
  • Association for Computing Machinery
  • The Innovator
  • Pioneers of Relentless and Innovative Storytellers of Multimedia Arts (PRISM)
  • Junior Philippine Computer Society
  • Mechanical Engineering Chain of Societies
